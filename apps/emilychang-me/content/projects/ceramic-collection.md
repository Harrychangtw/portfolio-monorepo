# Ceramic Collection

An exploration of tactile surfaces and organic forms through hand-built ceramics.

## About the Collection

This collection features a range of functional and decorative pieces, each uniquely crafted to celebrate imperfection and the beauty of handmade objects.

### Techniques Used

- Hand-building
- Coil construction
- Surface texturing
- Glaze experimentation
- Kiln firing

Each piece tells its own story through subtle variations in form, texture, and color.

# Urban Sketches

A visual diary of urban observations, drawn on location in various cities.

## About the Series

These sketches document moments of daily life, architecture, and human interactions in urban environments.

### Approach

- On-location drawing
- Quick gestural marks
- Focus on atmosphere
- Capturing movement

Each sketch is a snapshot of a fleeting moment, preserving the energy and character of the place.

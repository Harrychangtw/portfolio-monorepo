# Watercolor Studies

A collection of spontaneous watercolor paintings capturing fleeting moments and atmospheric effects.

## Series Description

These studies explore the interplay of water, pigment, and paper, embracing the unpredictable nature of the medium.

### Themes

- Light and shadow
- Natural landscapes
- Abstract forms
- Color harmony

The series celebrates the beauty of impermanence and the joy of experimentation.
